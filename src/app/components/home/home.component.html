What is Thrift?

Thrift is a platform committed to promoting financial maturity in individuals. A lot of poeple find it difficult to keep track of their spending, subsequently finding themselves overspending and blowing their budgets. Thrift was therefore created, as a tool to keep track of spending and enable savings.It aims to do all the hard work of keeping track of your expenses, helping you achieve your financial goals in the process.

What can Thrift do for you?

Record Expenses

Keep qualitative records of your daily spending. Say goodbye to trying and failing to remember what you spent on a particular day

Analyze your spending

Worried about overspending?, use our analytic tools to examine your spending

Report Generation

Generate and download spending reports. This can be in the form of a one time monthly report or for specified periods
